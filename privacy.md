# Privacy Policy – iNglishFrench

Last updated: July 2026

## Overview

iNglishFrench respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

## Data Collection

iNglishFrench does **not**:
- Collect personal data
- Require account registration
- Track users
- Use analytics or advertising services
- Access contacts, photos, or location

## Audio and Microphone Access

The app may request access to the microphone only for pronunciation practice.  
Audio is processed locally on your device and is **not recorded, stored, or transmitted**.

## Third-Party Services

iNglishFrench does not use third-party services that collect user data.

## Children’s Privacy

iNglishFrench does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If privacy practices change in future updates, this policy will be updated accordingly.
